Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping

We ship throughout India via trusted courier partners. Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days - ₹50
  • Express Shipping: 3-4 business days - ₹150
  • Free Shipping: On orders over ₹2000 (standard shipping only)

International Shipping

We currently ship to select international destinations. Please contact us before placing international orders.

  • Delivery times: 10-21 business days

  • Customs/duties are the responsibility of the recipient
